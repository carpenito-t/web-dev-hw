Easy Denver Omlete

Ingredients

Omlete:

  • Butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • Ham
  • Salt
  • Peppers
  • Onion
  • Cayanne

Directions

  1. Melt butter in pano F.

Filling

  1. Cut up onions and veg
  2. Cook onions veg and ham in pan to warm up
  3. mix all into egg after cooking in a different pan
