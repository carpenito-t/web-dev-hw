Step 4: Lighting your fire

If your fire isn't starting, make sure you have followed all of the above steps

  • light your tinder on fire using your fire source
  • once all of your sticks in your fire ring have caught fire, you can slowly add larger sticks
  • slowly increase the size of sticks you add
  • once you reach sticks about leg size, you will need fewer of them and they will burn more slowly

Step 5: Extinguishing your fire

Make sure to fully extinguish the fire before leaving your campsite

  • Let the fire die out on it's own until it is just smoldering ash
  • carefully and slowly pour your bucket of water over the ashes - it will still be very hot!
  • keep pouring water until you no longer see smoke
  • bury the puddle with some dirt
  • stir the mixture with your shovel until you no longer see smoke
  • continue this process if it is still warm, or you still see smoke
