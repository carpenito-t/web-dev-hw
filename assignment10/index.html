Best Fallout Games

Fallout New Vegas is a RPG developed by Bethesda. You play as a courrier who gets shot in the head at the beginging of the game. Your goal is to find out who tried to kill you and why. Takes place in a post apocolypic world after only a few survived in bunkers after an explosion.
Fallout 4 is the newest of the fallout games. This one by far has the best game play, but the story is not as good as Fallout New Vegas. You play as a vet who was frozen before the explosion. Your child is stolen in this new Fallout world and you have to find him.
